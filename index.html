Symptoms of DFUs

  • Redness or swelling in the foot
  • Open sores or wounds that are slow to heal
  • Pain, tingling, or numbness in the foot
  • Discoloration or skin changes

Types of Treatment

  • Wound care and dressings
  • Offloading pressure with specialized footwear
  • Antibiotic therapy for infections
  • Surgical intervention in severe cases
