Why Monitor Plantar Pressure in Diabetes?

In diabetic patients, peripheral neuropathy reduces pain sensation, allowing prolonged high plantar pressure to go unnoticed. This is one of the strongest predictors of foot ulcer formation and lower-limb amputation.

FootGuard continuously monitors plantar pressure patterns specific to this patient, identifying sustained mechanical stress before tissue breakdown occurs.
