Clinical Interpretation & Risk Analysis

Diabetes often leads to reduced sensation in the feet, allowing tissue damage to occur without pain. Elevated or sustained plantar pressure can cause skin breakdown and ulceration if not detected early.

Pressure Metrics Evaluated

  • Mean Pressure: Overall load on the foot
  • 90th Percentile Pressure: Sustained stress indicator
  • Peak Pressure: Acute overload detection
  • Trend Analysis: Identifies gradual risk increase

Machine Learning Interpretation

FootGuard applies a machine learning classifier trained using published diabetic foot pressure datasets. The model evaluates statistical features rather than raw sensor spikes to reflect real-world ulcer risk.
